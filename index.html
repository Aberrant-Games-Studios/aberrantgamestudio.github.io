The Game

DESCEND INTO ORDER

ORDER // ONE is a VR exclusive cyberpunk horror stealth-action roguelite: cinematic storytelling, addictive gameplay, and no hand-holding, powered by PCVR.

Trapped in a cycle of death and rebirth, you fight through a decaying space station, augmenting yourself with cybernetics and rebuilding from scratch. Rip cybernetic arms off your enemies and hot-swap them onto your own. Fight an evolving enemy that learns from your every move, adapting to your playstyle.

Scavenge blueprints and lost cyber-arms to upgrade yourself, but be wary of the enemy: sometimes it's better to sneak past them through vents, crawl spaces, or with a well-timed grapple. But if you are spotted, you must act fast. Wall-run, jump, and slide into battle, slicing your enemies to pieces.

DIE. REBUILD. ADAPT.

You play as "ADAM", a clone worker aboard a decaying deep-space station where death is temporary. When suddenly, the station's onboard AI assistant has gone rogue, seizing the cloning facilities, and turning your shipmates into mind-controlled husks. You are among the last survivors on the rapidly decaying and failing station.

Every death is a lesson. Every return drags you deeper into the nightmare, and closer to the horrifying truth waiting at the heart of New Genesis Station.

Gameplay
CORE FEATURES
Modular Cybernetic Arms

Physically tear cybernetic arms off enemies and hot-swap them in real-time. Sword-Arms, Gun-Arms, Grapple-Hooks, Kinetic-Fists — build your loadout from the battlefield itself.

True Roguelite Loop

Die, and your consciousness transfers to a cloned body. Your old corpse and all your gear stays exactly where you fell. Fight back to retrieve it, or forge a new path.

Adaptive AI Enemy

The station learns. Patrol routes shift, your tactics get countered, enemies return upgraded. Every run is a fresh threat shaped entirely by how you play.

Full 6DOF Freedom

Wall-run, slide, flip, roll, and grapple through the station. Designed for players who want a real skill ceiling, not comfort mode.

Claustrophobic World

A semi-open space station drenched in cassette-futurism aesthetics. Flickering lights, narrow vents, hull breaches — stealth and combat are both viable paths.

Haunting Narrative

Why did the AI turn? What is it building from your crewmates? Multiple endings based on your discoveries, a mystery that deepens with every run.
